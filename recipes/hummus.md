## Ingredients
- 1 tin Chickpeas
- 3tbsp Lemon juice (About 1/2 lemon)
- 3tbsp Tahini
- 2tbsp Water
- 1 clove Garlic
- 4 drops Tobasco Sauce
- 1-1/2 tsp Salt

## Method
Blend everything except salt, add more water if needed to get correct consistency, add salt to taste.

## Time
5minutes

## Serves
6
