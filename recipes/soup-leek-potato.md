## Ingredients
- 1oz (25g) Butter
- 1 Medium Onion
- 2 Large (800g) Leeks
- 3 medium (600g) Potato
- 1L Water
- 3 cubes (5g) Veg stock

## Method
Fry onions and leeks in butter. Add water, stock and potatoes, cook for 20 minutes then blend.
